Jeremy Nelson is the Metadata and Systems Librarian at Colorado College where for the past five years he has developed a number of open-source discovery and library linked-data systems cumulating in a current project he is doing for the Library of Congress; building a BIBFRAME-based catalog called bibcat, available at a public demo at http://bibcat.org/. Jeremy's primary research interest in developing a Catalog Pull Platform, which bibcat is a significant part of, that applies modern NoSQL and Web framework technologies to library bibliographic and digital assets management challenges. Jeremy has a BA from Knox College and a MS in Library and Information Science from the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign.
